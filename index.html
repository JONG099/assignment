GYMMBOXX BISHAN



ABOUT THE OUTLET

Commenced in 2015, GymmBoxx Bishan was the first 24-hours outlet of GymmBoxx gym.
Located in the heart of Singapore at Bishan, situated on the 4th level of Bishan Community Club, it offer everyone ease of commuting there, anytime of the day.
Apart from the spacious compound of 6,000 SQFT, they also provide premium and quality gym equipments.
Opened to public, you need not be a member in order to patronise and try out the gym for yourself.
