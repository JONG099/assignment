FACILITIES





FACILITIES AT A GLANCE

Apart from the usual gym equipments, machines and accessories, shower facilities are also available here.
GymmBoxx also provide premium equipments and personalise training programmes.
Unlike most private gym, you have the option of pay-per entry. So if you are unsure if you would like the gym, you can give it a try!
To top it off, they are open 24/7 and have 6,000 SQFT of workout area making sure you don't get packed like sardines & tuna.
